Addiction Dice rules

Materials needed:

  • One six sided di
  • Pieces of Paper for each person
  • Writing utensils for each person

Each person starts by taking their piece of paper and numbering it from 1-30. Each player takes turns rolling the di. If the di lands on 1 the round is over and the score is reset to 0. If the di lands on 2 you double the score. For any other number add the said number to the score.
